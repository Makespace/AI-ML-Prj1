Domino Joiner
Practical Assessment 

In the practical assessment:
You can ask any questions you may have from the training
The trainer will show you how to make a simple domino joint.
Then you will show the trainer you can use the Domino Joiner safely by:
Cutting and dry fitting an end to face 90° corner joint.
Changing the cutter.
Cutting and dry fitting an end to face (e.g. leg to table top) joint.
