DO
Visually inspect the unit and charger before use to check for faults.
Use the supplied charger. 
Use the wrist strap to prevent damage to the unit. 
Switch the unit off after use and store safety away in the storage box. 
DO NOT 
Drop and damage the unit. 
Point the imager at the sun or other intense energy sources.
If in doubt: switch off the unit
Report dangerous faults to the group and Management and label the item ‘Faulty: Do not use’. 
Electric shock hazard; burns hazard; fire hazard
ORANGE EQUIPMENT: Training optional, but you must feel confident in the use of the device and must have read the equipment instructions
Use of the wrist strap is advised
