Practical Assessment

What you will do
Slice a simple model using Cura on makespace computer
Feel free to try this at home beforehand
Print model (maximum of 10 minute print time) in PLA
Load and unload filament
Remove print from bed
Place filament into storage
Weigh your print (paying is on the house for this one ^^)

You can bring a model-file (STL) you want to print for a project to discuss slicing settings, design considerations etc. with the trainer! This is not mandatory.
