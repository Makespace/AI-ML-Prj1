2) Setting up the Model Mill: SCOPE
Before you start:
Consider materials and tools
Watch the videos cited in the Resources slide below
Set-up Tasks:
Switch Model Mill On (& Off)
Emergency Stop & View button
Install Milling bit in spindle head
Mount Work piece on table
Set spindle head X, Y and Z coordinates
Level surface of work piece
You are now ready to cut
