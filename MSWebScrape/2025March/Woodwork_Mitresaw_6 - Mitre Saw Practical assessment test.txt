Mitre Saw
Practical Assessment 

In the practical assessment:
You can ask any questions you may have from the training
The trainer will show you how to cut safely with the mitre saw
Then you will show the trainer you can use the mitre saw safely by making
a straight cut in wood
an angled cut in wood
any cut in a material other than wood (optional)
