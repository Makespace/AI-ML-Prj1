Woodworking tools
Practical Assessment 

In the practical assessment:
You can ask any questions you may have from the training
The trainer will give you an overview of the tools available
Then you will show the trainer you can use the handtools safely by:
Sharpening a hand plane blade (including disassembly and reassembly)
Sharpening a chisel 
Using the right saw for ripping or cross-cutting
