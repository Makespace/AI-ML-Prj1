In conclusion
This training guide is a BASIC introduction to simple 2D milling
PRACTICE using the Model Mill and related software as soon as possible after completing the training 
Expect to commit at least 4 hours to practicing in order to ingrain the basics
If in doubt, STOP and ask
If something on the Model Mill breaks, or something appears unsafe, STOP working immediately and inform cncmodelmill@makespace.org
Do not attempt to repair it yourself
Enjoy!


In conclusion
If a milling bit breaks do not worry. Inform cncmodelmill@makespace.org
