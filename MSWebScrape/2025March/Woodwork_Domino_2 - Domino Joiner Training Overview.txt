Domino Joiner
What you will get from this training

Scope of the training
These online materials are designed to get you ready to operate the Makespace Domino Joiner DF500 with wood or MDF materials.
No prior knowledge of how to use a Domino Joiner is assumed.
If you already know how to use a Domino or biscuit joiner, this material has been designed to get you up to speed with the DF500 quickly. Please be patient with the process and if you have any tips to improve this guide, let us know!
This guide covers what all Domino Joiner users need to know in Makespace.

Learning objectives
How the Domino Joiner works
How to use it safely
How to check the condition of the machine so it is safe and cuts well
What Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) is needed and why
How to inspect the Domino Joiner before use
How to set up the Domino Joiner to cut surface, edge, end or mitre mortices.
Cleaning-up up after use

This training material gives you a complete overview of the knowledge we expect all users to be familiar with.
It also gives tips and suggestions on how to get good results.
You need to complete the Domino Joiner quiz, and the MakeSpace general safety quiz when ready.
Once you have passed the quizzes, you can arrange to have in-person assessment. Only once you have been shown person-to-person can you use the Domino Joiner.
