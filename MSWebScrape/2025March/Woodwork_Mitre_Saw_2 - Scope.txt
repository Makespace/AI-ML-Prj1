Mitre Saw
What you will get from this training

Scope of the training
These online materials are designed to get you ready to operate the Makespace Evolution Rage 3DB mitre saw with wood, metal, and plastic materials.
No prior knowledge of how to use a mitre saw is assumed.
If you already know how to use other chop saws, this material has been designed to get you up to speed with the Rage 3DB quickly. Please be patient with the process and if you have any tips to improve this guide, let us know!
This guide covers what all mitre saw users need to know in Makespace.

Learning objectives
How the mitre saw works
How to use it safely
How to check the condition of the machine so it is safe and cuts well
What Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) is needed and why
How to inspect the mitre saw before use
How to set up the mitre saw to cut mitre, bevel, and compound cuts.
How to cut wood, metal, and plastic materials with the mitre saw
Cleaning-up up after use

This training material gives you a complete overview of the knowledge we expect all users to be familiar with.
It also gives tips and suggestions on how to get good results
You need to complete the mitre saw quiz, and the workshop safety quiz when ready
Once you have passed the quizzes, you can arrange to have in-person assessment and only once you have been shown person-to-person can you use the mitre saw.
