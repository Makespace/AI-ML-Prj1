Biological Safety Cabinet
Practical Assessment 
Includes assessment for aseptic and safe use of the CO2 incubators

In the practical assessment:
You can ask any questions you may have from the training
Then you will 
1. show the trainer you can switch the Biological Safety Cabinet on and off 
2. explain best practice and show how to use the safety cabinet
3. explain how to deal with a biological spill
4. explain and show how to use the CO2 incubators
