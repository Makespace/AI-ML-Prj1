Checks Before First Use
If you are the first user of the day (or if you are unsure), oil the lower bobbin mechanism.

Checks Before Each Operation
The correct frame is properly attached.
The fabric is mounted tightly in the frame.
The correct threads are present and the needles are correctly threaded.
When Operating This Equipment You MUST
Remain with the machine, YOU are responsible for ensuring that anyone (including children) who come into the room do not injure themselves by touching the moving needles.
Sharp needles.
