DO
Visually inspect the charger before use to check for faults.
Switch the unit off after use and charge. 
DO NOT
Use the unit when charging. 
Connect voltage sources e.g. charged capacitors. 
Drop the or damage the unit / probes. 
Attempt to swap the lithium ion battery (report faults).
If in doubt: switch off the unit
Report dangerous faults to the group and Management and label the item ‘Faulty: Do not use’. 
Electric shock hazard from charger
GREEN EQUIPMENT: Low risk of personal injury. Understand safe operation & read the risk assessments.
