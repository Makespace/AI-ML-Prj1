Checks Before First Use
The plunge action is free and smooth
The blade is not damaged and turns freely
The riving knife is in place and emerges with the blade
The case is not damaged
The power cord is not damaged

Checks Before Each Operation
The festool dust extractor is connected, powered on and set to AUTO
The track is in place and the blade is clear of the workpiece
The workpiece is secured and supported, the kerf will not close during the cut
When making a plunge cut a stop is in place to prevent kick-back

When Operating This Equipment You MUST
Hold the machine firmly with two hands, when making a bevel cut place one hand on the base plate to prevent tipping
Plunge the saw slowly and steadily, always cut away from yourself, push slowly and gently, let the saw do the work
Hold the hose and power cord over one arm if necessary to prevent them snagging on the end of the track
Ensure the machine has fully stopped before removing the saw from the track
Sharp blade; dust hazard, mask recommended; noise hazard, ear protection required
RED EQUIPMENT: Not to be used without completing online training and practical assessment
Eye and ear protection required, dust mask required when cutting MDF or Irritant Wood
