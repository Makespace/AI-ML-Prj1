Plunge Saw
Practical Assessment 

In the practical assessment:
You can ask any questions you may have from the training
The trainer will show you how to set up a
thin strip cut off the edge of the bench
measured (rip) cut using the track and mft bench dogs
square length (cross) cut.
Then you will show the trainer you can use the Plunge Saw by making these cuts.
If you are interested the trainer may also demonstrate the use of Jigs on the MFT, this is not required training to use the saw.
